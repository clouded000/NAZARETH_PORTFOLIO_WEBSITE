INTRODUCTION

Hi! I’m Megan Villadelgado Nazareth, a third-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Quezon City Branch. I’m 20 years old, born on March 1, 2005, and I currently live in North Fairview, Quezon City.

As a student, I’m deeply interested in exploring how technology can be used creatively to solve real-world problems and make everyday life easier. I enjoy learning new concepts, experimenting with different tools, and improving my skills through both academic and personal projects.

Aside from my studies, I’m also an entrepreneur and the owner of Clouded Threads, a small business where I handcraft crochet trinkets. Crocheting allows me to express my creativity and patience, and it’s a relaxing way to bring something beautiful to life from just yarn and imagination.

You might also come across the name “Kumo” — that’s my pseudonym, inspired by my love for clouds and the sky. “Kumo” reflects my creative side and reminds me to stay light, curious, and open to endless possibilities — whether in technology, art, or life.
